Part 2 of the PostgreSQL 18 series covers security updates including deprecation of MD5 password authentication in favor of more secure methods, monitoring enhancements for parallel workers and replication conflict tracking, the new uuidv7() function for timestamp-ordered identifiers, and logical replication improvements on Amazon Aurora and Amazon RDS for PostgreSQL.
