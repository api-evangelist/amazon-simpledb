Amazon ElastiCache now supports aggregation queries, making it easier to filter, group, transform, and summarize data directly in your cache with a single query. You can use aggregation queries to build real-time application experiences with latencies as low as microseconds over terabytes of data and results reflecting completed writes. Analyze the data where it already lives, at the speed your application demands, without a separate analytics layer.
