Amazon RDS for Db2 now supports IBM Db2 12.1, the latest generation of the Db2 database engine. Alongside this upgrade, we’re introducing a new edition: Community Edition (db2-ce). You now have three edition choices when you provision an Amazon RDS for Db2 instance.
